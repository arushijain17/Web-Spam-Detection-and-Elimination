Things to do in Delhi

While discussing the things to do in Delhi, most people have a tough time deciding which of the Delhi things to do may be skipped. For nocturnal beings, a couple of popular haunts are F Bar and Lounge and Blue Frog. Shopaholics may get spoilt for choice at Connaught Place, Khan Market, and Chor Bazaar. And yes, if foodies wonder about what to do in Delhi, they may find the answer at places such as Dumpukht, China Kitchen, Pan Asian, and Cafe Diva to name a few.
